## ◼ About Me

I'm **Saransh Sharma**, a Computer Science Engineering student at **Medicaps University, Indore**, shipping real products from day one. I focus on **backend engineering**, **AI-powered applications**, and **agentic automation** — the kind of software that solves actual problems at actual scale.

🏆 Won **1st Place at the CodeCanvas National Hackathon**, got selected for the offline round at **CMR University (AICTE & ProAI)**, and cleared two government hackathon rounds — **SIH** and **MSME 5.0**.
